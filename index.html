Wage

An inflationary, decentralized store of value, run by its union of holders.

Buy Wage Telegram

Wage bestows holders a mutable, transactionless wage. Wage (initially) mints a new token every three hours, proportionally distributing it to all holders. There is no need to stake, claim, or do anything to receive your wage.

Inflation and emission are to be voted on by holders through the Union, the decentralized autonomous organization of the Wage project. All future dapps built on the Wage platform will utilize Wage as a payment token.

At genesis, Wage's inflation rate is 1 new token every 3 hours, with the inflation turned off. Inflation will commence once the first vote on telegram (quasi-governance) passes, and will remain unchanged until the Union launch.

Wage's inflationary mechanic is broken down into three components:

  1. Rate: How often a new Wage is minted.
  2. Amount: The amount of Wage minted per emission.
  3. State: The ability to pause and resume Wage emissions.

Non-dilutive Inflation Even as the supply of Wage increases, you will always own the same piece of the pie.

All holders of Wage are part of the Union. The Union is the governance platform for Wage where holders can propose changes to emissions, inflation, and revise the fee models of upcoming dapps on the Wage platform.

Unlike typical systems of governance where votes are often 1:1 with token holdings, the Union incorporates quadratic voting. That is, x:sqrtx will be the way in which votes are counted. Say farewell to the reign of whales.
